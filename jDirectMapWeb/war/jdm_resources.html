Academic

 

The Institute of Software Technology and Interactive Systems (ISIS) is within the Faculty of Computer Science of the Vienna University of Technology. It consists of 4 different working groups: Information and Software Group (IFS), Interactive Media Systems Group (IMS), Electronic Commerce Group (EC), and Business Informatics Group (BIG). The focus of the institute is on software engineering, e-commerce, model-driven development, Semantic Web technologies, and interactive systems. In addition to teaching, the institute is engaged in many international and national research and development programs, both in basic and applied research. Besides that, it also provides consulting services in the fields mentioned.



The jDirectMap project will be supported and implemented under supervision of both the Electronic Commerce Group and Business Informatics Group
mainly Philipp Liegl, Marco Zapletal as well as others.

The Electronic Commerce Group (EC) takes a broader view at e-commerce processes: it analyzes enterprises and their processes as well as the entire market, and it is active in creating ideas and solutions that meet the demands of the respective stakeholders. In the B2B domain business (processes) modeling and consequently the definition, specification, and implementation of ebusiness systems are at the focus of its research. In the domain of Semantic Web it specializes on ontology engineering and information integration in order to enhance current e-commerce solutions.
